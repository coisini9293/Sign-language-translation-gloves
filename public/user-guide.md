# Sign Language Glove User Guide

## Usage Instructions

### 1. Wearing the Device

Wear the device on your forearm, ensuring:
- Sensor patches make good contact with your fingers
- Device fits snugly on your wrist
- All connections are secure

### 2. Power On

- Press the power button on the side of the device to start
- After startup, the device will automatically enter gesture recognition mode
- Wait for the startup indicator to confirm the device is ready

### 3. Gesture Input Instructions

**Important Notes:**
- The device automatically samples gesture data every **2 seconds**
- When making gestures, ensure your fingers start from a **relaxed state**
- Make the target gesture clearly
- Hold the gesture for approximately **1 second**
- Wait for voice feedback confirmation

**Gesture Recognition Process:**
1. Start with fingers in a fully relaxed position
2. Smoothly transition to the target gesture
3. Hold the gesture steady for about 1 second
4. Listen for voice output confirmation

### 4. Troubleshooting Recognition Issues

If a gesture is not correctly recognized:

**Step 1:** Return fingers to a fully relaxed state
- Ensure all fingers are completely extended
- Relax your hand naturally

**Step 2:** Repeat the gesture
- Perform the gesture slightly slower
- Make the gesture more pronounced
- Ensure sensor patches maintain good contact

**Step 3:** Device restart (if needed)
- If recognition continues to fail, try restarting the device
- Press and hold the power button to turn off
- Wait 3 seconds, then power on again

### 5. Charging & Maintenance

#### Charging
- **Port Type:** Type-C interface
- **Charging Time:** Approximately 2 hours for full charge
- **Charging Indicator:** LED light shows charging status
  - Red: Charging in progress
  - Green: Fully charged

#### Maintenance
- **Sensor Patches:**
  - Removable and washable
  - Clean with mild soap and water
  - Allow to dry completely before reattaching
  - **WARNING:** Do not immerse the main unit in water

- **Main Unit Care:**
  - Wipe with a slightly damp cloth
  - Do not expose to excessive moisture
  - Store in a dry place when not in use

#### Firmware Upgrade
- Connect to computer via Type-C port
- Download the latest firmware from our official website
- Follow the upgrade software instructions
- Do not disconnect during the upgrade process

## Safety Precautions

1. Do not use the device in extremely humid environments
2. Keep away from high temperatures and direct sunlight
3. Do not attempt to disassemble the main unit
4. Use only the provided charging cable
5. If you experience skin irritation, discontinue use and consult a physician
